# Canon Map — Stunspot's Guide to Sales Prospecting Strategy

This canon maps B2B prospecting as a commercial reasoning system: market pressure becomes institutional demand only when evidence, priority, budget legitimacy, buyer-system capacity, risk mitigation, and execution discipline align.

The sequence matters. Early reports define the reality model and evidence rules; middle reports map buyer systems, signals, stakeholders, and messaging; later reports cover outbound operations, CRM/RAG memory, handoff integrity, funnel analytics, and failure intervention.

## Sequence Logic

```text
Commercial reality
  -> macro-financial constraint
  -> signal and evidence discipline
  -> account and buyer-system mapping
  -> signal discovery and interpretation
  -> ICP and market prioritization
  -> buyer psychology and stakeholder politics
  -> procurement and risk mitigation
  -> message engineering and channel systems
  -> commercial memory and AE handoff
  -> funnel economics
  -> failure diagnosis and intervention
```
