# Status

*Stunspot's Guide to Sales Prospecting Strategy* is a **v1.0 canon release** built from an existing model-authored knowledge corpus and packaged for public AI/RAG use.

## Release State

- Version: **1.0**
- Release date: **2026-06-28**
- Source reports: **16**
- Compiled packs: **5**
- Omnibus files: **1**
- License: **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0**

## Maturity

This is a complete first public release of the canon package. The source reports, compiled packs, omnibus bundle, manifest, citation metadata, and navigation docs are present and aligned to the repository’s directory policy.

The corpus is suitable for:

- AI/RAG project knowledge
- long-context commercial reasoning
- sales prospecting strategy analysis
- outbound system design
- demand-signal interpretation
- buyer-system mapping
- GTM failure diagnosis

## Directory Policy

- Source reports are canonical individual units: `knowledge-packs/by-report/`
- Compiled packs are convenience upload formats: `knowledge-packs/compiled-packs/`
- The omnibus is the full-corpus convenience bundle: `knowledge-packs/omnibus/`
- `docs/` is navigation and usage guidance, not the source-report corpus.

## Known Limitations

- Hero and social image references are intentionally retained as placeholders until final brand assets are created.
- The corpus is model-authored and should be treated as a structured research and strategy aid, not as a substitute for current primary-source verification.
- High-impact strategic, financial, legal, compliance, or revenue decisions should be checked against current context and human judgment.

## Current Positioning

This repository is primarily model-facing. It is human-readable, but its first job is to give AI assistants stable terminology, report sequence, evidence discipline, source traceability, and operational doctrine for sales prospecting strategy.
